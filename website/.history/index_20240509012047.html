Welcome to CEDE

CEDE stands for Consensus for Experimental Design in Electromyography. It is an international initiative to guide decision-making in recording, analysis, and interpretation of electromyography (EMG). Learn more about CEDE

This is the XYZ tool, created to translate scientific recommendations on EMG. You can use it by selecting one of the topics below and experiment criteria to work through the decision tree, and arrive at a recommended appropriate method to apply. Learn more about this tool
